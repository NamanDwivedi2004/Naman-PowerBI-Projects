# üîç Key Insights
- National Outcome: The NDA Alliance secured a majority with 292 seats (54%), forming the government, while the I.N.D.I.A. Alliance won 234 seats (43%).
- Role of Others: Smaller parties and independents collectively secured 17 seats (3%), influencing close contests in select regions.
- Rajasthan Performance: In Rajasthan, the NDA won 14 seats, outperforming the I.N.D.I.A. alliance with 11 seats, indicating strong regional dominance.
- Constituency Highlight ‚Äì Kota:
- Winner: Om Birla (BJP) with 750,496 votes
- Runner-up: Prahlad Gunjal (INC) with 708,522 votes
- NOTA Votes: 10,261
- Voting Method Insight: A significant majority of votes were cast via EVMs, with postal votes forming a smaller but notable share.
- Party Dominance: BJP remains the single largest party within NDA, contributing the majority of alliance seats nationwide.
- Regional Trends: Northern and western states largely favored the NDA, while parts of southern and eastern India showed stronger support for the I.N.D.I.A. alliance.
- High Competition Zones: Several constituencies witnessed close margins, highlighting competitive political landscapes and voter engagement.

# üîë Key Features
- National Overview: Displays total seats (543) with alliance-wise seat share for NDA, I.N.D.I.A., and Others.
- Constituency-Level Analysis: Detailed breakdown of votes, EVM vs postal votes, total candidates, and winner/runner-up details for selected constituencies.
- State Political Landscape: State-wise seat distribution by alliance and party, with interactive maps and tabular insights.
- Alliance Comparison: Clear comparison between NDA and I.N.D.I.A. alliances across states.
- Winning Candidate Insights: Constituency-level visualization of winning candidates and their respective parties.
- Demographic & Geographic Analysis: Interactive maps highlighting majority seats, alliance dominance, and regional political trends.
- Party-Wise Seat Share: Donut and tabular visuals showing party contribution within alliances.
- Interactive Filters: Filters for state and constituency selection enable focused and customized analysis.
